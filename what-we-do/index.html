Specialist Eyebrows Threading

Threading is the process of spinning thread over hair, causing the hair to wrap around the string, subsequently pulling the hair out from the root. Unlike tweezing, where single hairs are pulled out one at a time, threading can remove an entire row of hair at once. Threading is terrific if your skin is sensitive. It’s also a great option if you are using prescription medications or if you use retinol as it doesn’t create any pull of the skin itself.
